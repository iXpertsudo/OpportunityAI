Opportunity Application Guide

Expert tips and strategies to help you land your dream scholarship, internship, or hackathon victory.

Crafting the Perfect Essay

Scholarship and internship essays are where you truly stand out. AI can help you brainstorm, but your essay should remain deeply personal.

  • Tell a Story: Don't just list achievements; frame them around a compelling narrative of overcoming a challenge or discovering a passion.
  • Be Specific: Instead of "I like AI," write "I built a machine learning model to predict local crop yields."
  • Address the Prompt: Ensure every paragraph serves the specific questions asked by the committee.

Winning Hackathons

Hackathons are less about having perfectly clean code and more about executing a brilliant, complete presentation in 48 hours.

  • The 80/20 Rule: 80% of your points come from the core feature and the pitch. Don't waste 10 hours on a login screen.
  • API Integrations: Use powerful APIs (like OpenAI or Twilio) to easily add "wow" factor features.
  • The Pitch is King: Practice your 3-minute demo thoroughly. Create a clear narrative: Problem -> Solution -> Demo -> Future Impact.
